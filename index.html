My name is Soraya Scrofani I am 20 years old and my work consists of concept design, I love being flexible when it comes to art as it allows me to adapt to any style I can to fit the aquired target audience. I like to draw characters, whatever I draw varies on inspiration and mood but I can range from humans, to animals to even objects as characters. I always believed that this field can be very healing for the inner child and it also expands creativity and imagination.
